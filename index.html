Dog Info...

Learn about different dog breeds and their characteristics.

..Family-Friendly Dogs..

Labrador Retriever

Very friendly: Loves people, strangers, and other animals

Loyal & loving: Strong bonding with family
Intelligent: Learns commands quickly

Golden Retriever

Extremely friendly: Loves people, strangers, and other pets

Loyal & loving: Strong bonding with family
Intelligent: Learns commands quickly

Very gentle: Soft-natured and patient, ideal for children

Beagle

Extremely friendly: Loves people, strangers, and other pets

Intelligent but stubborn: Learns fast, but follows nose more than commands

Playful & energetic: Always active and curious

Pug

Very affectionate Loves human company and attention

Playful & funny: Clown-like, entertaining personality
Good with kids: Gentle and patient

Calm indoors:Low energy compared to larger breeds

Indian Spitz

Friendly & affectionate: Loves family members and kids

Energetic & playful: Active, enjoys running and playing
Loyal: Strong attachment to owner

Alert & watchful:Good little watchdog, barks on strangers

..Small Dog Breeds..

Pomeranian

Friendly & affectionate:Loves attention from family

Energetic & playful: Small but very active
Intelligent: Learns commands quickly

Alert & vocal:Makes a good little watchdog, barks at strangers

Chihuahua

Affectionate & loyal: Forms strong bond with owner

Energetic & lively: Small size but very active
Intelligent: Learns commands quickly

Alert & protective: Can bark at strangers, good tiny watchdog

Shih Tzu

Very affectionate: Loves cuddles and human company

Calm & playful: Enjoys short play sessions but mostly calm indoors
Intelligent: Learns commands quickly

Playful & energetic: Always active and curious

Dachshund

Affectionate & loyal:: Very attached to owner and family

Bold & confident: Fearless personality despite small size
Playful & energetic: Loves games and short walks

Alert & vocal:Barks at strangers

Jack Russell Terrier

Very energetic: Needs lots of exercise and play

Highly intelligent: Learns quickly, very sharp mind
Bold & fearless:Confident, strong personality

Strong hunting instinct:Loves chasing and digging

..large Dog Breeds..

Siberian Husky

Very friendly:Loves people and other dogs

Playful & energetic: Needs lots of exercise and space
Highly intelligent: Smart but very independent

Stubborn nature:Training needs patience and consistency

Alaskan Malamute

Very friendly & affectionate: Loves family and people

Calm but powerful: More calm than Husky, but very strong
Highly intelligent: Smart, but thinks independently

Stubborn: Needs firm, consistent

Great Dane

Gentle & calm: Known as a “gentle giant”

Very friendly:Loves people, kids, and family
Affectionate: Enjoys attention and closeness

Loyal:Forms strong bond with owners

Saint Bernard

Very gentle & calm:Famous for sweet and patient nature

Extremely friendly: Loves family, kids, and strangers
Affectionate: Enjoys human company and attention

Low aggression:Rarely aggressive

Rottweiler

Loyal & devoted: Extremely protective of family

Confident & courageous: Strong, fearless personality
Intelligent:Learns commands quickly with proper training

Calm & steady:Not hyper without reason
